📡 OPERATOR PROFILE
Michael (M7OMH)
Foundation Licence Operator • West Yorkshire
I’m a UK Foundation amateur radio operator based in West Yorkshire. I operate across HF, VHF, and UHF, with a strong interest in both local contacts and long-distance propagation.

On HF, I enjoy experimenting with FT8 and FT4, alongside traditional voice modes when conditions allow. On VHF/UHF, I operate local FM and digital modes such as DMR.

I’m also active with the MeshCore network, helping to connect local nodes and explore resilient off-grid communications systems.

This site is a simple station hub for logging activity, experiments, and sharing progress in the hobby.
